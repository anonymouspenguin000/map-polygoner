Polygons

    About

    In Polygoner you can select areas on the map and then export them as polygons.

    Click on a place on the map to place a pin there, click on that pin to remove it. Two pins create a polygon (rectangle); press [Enter] to place the polygon on the map, it will also appear in the sidebar list. You can add as many polygons as you want. You can select a polygon by clicking it on the map or in the list (you can't do that if there are pins on the map). Press [Delete] to remove the selected polygon ([Shift+Delete] to prevent confirmation). Press [Escape] to deselect pins or polygons.
